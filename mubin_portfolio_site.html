Selected Research Projects

Multimodal Autism Detection (Published)

Developing a deep learning system that combines facial images and behavioral features to detect ASD in children; published at IEEE ICCIT 2024},

Hematology Causal AI Study

Hematology-based inflammatory risk detection using 20,000+ blood samples collected from multiple institutions. This work involves feature selection with SHAP-based explainability integrated.

Diabetes Risk Prediction (Ongoing)

Diabetes risk prediction using XGBoost, where we are collecting clinical data from several hospitals for model development and interpretation.

Lung Function Decline Estimation in Smokers (Ongoing)

We are also investigating lung-function decline in smokers using ensemble models and a custom ANN that achieved ~99% accuracy. These projects are strengthening my research competence in medical data modeling.

Dental Scaling Assessment (Planned)

Designing a computer vision pipeline for tooth segmentation to evaluate dental plaque and scaling needs (design & literature review stage).
